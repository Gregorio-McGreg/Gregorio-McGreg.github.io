Ode to Loger Taper Fade

Logan walks with a rhythm all his own,
But you can call him Loger when the mood has grown.
Smooth as a breeze on a cool spring parade,
They whisper Loger-Taper-Fade, crisp as a fade.

When the world gets loud, and the crowd starts to sway,
He slips into the shadows, Lo-key all the way.
A quiet force, like thunder in sleep,
Carrying secrets the wild wind keep.

But to those who know him, and call him a friend,
It’s Logee, the name that softens the end.
A laugh in the dark, a spark in the cold,
A story of courage that's yet to be told.

By many names, he dances through days,
A chameleon soul in a sun-dappled haze.
Logan, Loger, or Lo-Key in shade,
You’ll always remember Loger-Taper-Fade.
